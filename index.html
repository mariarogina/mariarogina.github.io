About

I am a highly educated (Veliky Novgorod University, Linguistics 2009) and experienced (11 year by 2020) linguist. Open to collaboration with those who need a high-quality translation.

I am especially interested in translating medical, technical, legal, financial and personal documents. However, I can work with texts on almost any topic, which is negotiable. Here is the link to my LinkedIn page: https://www.linkedin.com/in/maria-ro/
